# ISSUE-005: Cramped Header Statistics

## Status: Open
## Priority: Low
## Category: UI / Polish

### Description
The "Ducks" and "Packets" statistics displayed in the main application header are positioned too close to each other. They lack proper margin/padding and distinct visual separation, making them look like a single string of text rather than distinct metrics.

### Steps to Reproduce
1. Log in.
2. Look at the header bar.
3. Observe the Duck and Packet counters.

### Expected Behavior
Metrics should be clearly separated, perhaps with icons, badges, or at least 1-2rem of spacing.

### Actual Behavior
The numbers and labels are cramped together.
