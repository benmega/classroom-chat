# Achievements Page Lacks Progress Tracking

## Description
The Achievements page (`/achievements`) displays a grid of achievement badges but lacks any progress tracking or contextual information about overall completion. This makes the page feel less functional compared to the "Course Progress" section on the main Profile page.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Log in as a student.
2. Navigate to the Achievements page.
3. Observe the list of badges.

## Expected Result
Detailed progress bars or stats for specific categories (e.g., "CodeCombat Progress", "Projects Completed") similar to those on the Profile page.

## Actual Result
The page only shows earned vs. locked badges with minimal tooltips.

## Impact
Medium - Reduces the utility of the Achievements page for students tracking their long-term progress.
