# Admin User Management Modals Narrow and Clipped

## Description
Modals in the Admin "User Management" section (e.g., "Create New User", "Adjust Duck Balance") are extremely narrow and appear cut off on the right side. The layout of the modal content is constrained, making it difficult to interact with form fields and buttons.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Navigate to `/admin`.
2. Click on "Users" in the sidebar.
3. Click on the "Add User" button.
4. Observe the modal width and the clipped content on the right.

## Expected Result
Modals should have sufficient width and padding to display form content clearly without clipping.

## Actual Result
The modal is very narrow, and the right side of the form content is cut off.

## Impact
Major - This makes it extremely difficult for administrators to manage users or perform balance adjustments.
